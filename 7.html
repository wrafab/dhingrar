The SkateBoard
The skateboard involved using the cross linked and the longitudinal wood peices. Gluing the peices together was a real challenge as the wood would dry and the skateboard would not take its shape.
The seven minute constraint forced us to work in pairs to get the peices together glued in the vaccum bag. The air was sucked out to curve the sides of the board.
